The job

Job title: Junior Web Developer.

You will be joining a dedicated team developing your own skills and learning new skills within the Web Development department.

Key responsibilities:

  • Deliver technical support to keep the business successful.
  • Be able to problem solve.
  • Help the development and applications of coding standards.
  • Maintain technical awareness.

Our workplace is located at 123 Street. We need cooporative, hard working web developers to aid in developing dream websites.

Required qualifications

  • Experience working in a computer science job.
  • Proficient in HTML, CSS and Javascript.
  • Examples of previous work.
  • English Level 3.
  • Maths Level 3.
  • Web Development Course.
